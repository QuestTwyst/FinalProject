About Questwyst

A story that bends around your choices. Questwyst is a creative storytelling project built around one idea: reading should feel like deciding, not just following along. Every scene ends at a fork — two paths, two consequences — and the story you finish is the one only you chose.

How it works. Each Questwyst story is a short sequence of scenes. At the end of every scene, you're handed two options — side by side, no hierarchy between them — and your pick carries you into the next moment of the story.

Read. A short paragraph sets the scene — a place, a mood, a decision waiting to happen.

Choose. Option A on one side, option B on the other. Neither is the "correct" one — each just leads somewhere different.

Branch. Your choice changes the backdrop and the stakes. No two readers necessarily land on the same page twice in a row.

Replay. Every story is short by design, so going back and choosing differently is part of the fun, not a chore.

Why "Questwyst." The name is exactly what it sounds like: a quest — a journey with a purpose — bent by a twist at every step. It's a small promise about what you're getting into: a story that moves, and moves because of you.
